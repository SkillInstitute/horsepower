Product Benefits & Description

Unani Swarna Bhasma, which is gold ash, has been traditionally used in Unani medicine for its potential health benefits. Some of the benefits associated with Swarna Bhasma include:

Rejuvenating Properties

Swarna Bhasma is believed to have rejuvenating properties that can help in revitalizing the body and mind.

Vitality Booster

It is thought to be a potent vitality booster, helping in enhancing overall energy levels and stamina.

Antioxidant Rich

Swarna Bhasma is rich in antioxidants, which may help in reducing oxidative stress and combating free radicals.

Anti-inflammatory

It is believed to possess anti-inflammatory properties, which can aid in reducing inflammation and associated symptoms.

Immune Support

Some traditional practices suggest that Swarna Bhasma helps in strengthening the immune system.

Nervous System Support

It is also believed to have a positive impact on the nervous system, potentially aiding cognitive function.
